## 1. Account Creation & Management

### Creating an Account

1. Open Mahjong Club and tap **Create Account**.
2. Enter your **first name**, **last name**, **email address**, and a **password** (at least 6 characters).
3. Optionally choose an **avatar** — pick from a curated collection of mahjong tiles, flowers, cocktails, and luxury icons.
4. Tap **Create Account** to finish.

> **Tip:** You can also sign in instantly with **Sign in with Google** — no password needed.

---

### Signing In

1. Open the app and tap **Sign In**.
2. Enter your email and password, then tap **Sign In**.
3. Alternatively, tap **Sign in with Google** to use your Google account.

---

### Signing Out

1. Tap **Account** in the bottom navigation.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Sign Out**.

---

### Resetting Your Password

1. On the Sign In screen, tap **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email address and tap the send button.
3. Check your inbox — you'll receive a password reset email shortly.

> The app never confirms whether an email is registered, for your privacy.

---

### Updating Your Profile

1. Tap **Account** in the bottom navigation.
2. In the **My Profile** card, tap **Edit ✏️**.
3. Update your **Display Name**, **Skill Level**, **Timezone**, or **Avatar**.
4. Tap **Save Changes ✨** when done.

---

### Changing Your Avatar

1. Tap **Account** → **Edit ✏️** in the My Profile card.
2. Scroll down to the **Avatar** section.
3. Tap any avatar to select it.
4. Tap **Save Changes ✨**.

---

### Managing Your Subscription

1. Tap **Account** in the bottom navigation.
2. In the **Subscription** card, review your current plan and usage.
3. Tap **Manage Subscription →** to view plan options or upgrade.

Your subscription shows how many groups and upcoming hosted games you've used against your plan's limits.

## 2. Scheduling & Games

### Creating a Game

Games can be created from inside a group or as a standalone game (no group required).

**From a group:**
1. Tap **Groups** → tap your group.
2. Tap **🀄 Schedule a Game**.
3. Fill in the game details (see fields below).
4. Tap **Schedule Game**.

**From Home or the + button:**
1. Tap the **+** button (or **Schedule new game** from the menu).
2. Choose a group or select **No group (standalone)**.
3. Fill in the game details and tap **Schedule Game**.

**Game fields:**
- **Game Title** — e.g., "Weekly Game Night"
- **Date** — pick from the calendar
- **Start / End Time** — set when the game begins and ends
- **Location** — address or venue name
- **Tables** — each table seats 4 players (tap +/− to adjust)
- **Host Notes** (optional) — house rules, what to bring, style of play

---

### Inviting Group Members When Scheduling

While creating a game, a list of your group members appears below the form. Tap a member to invite them — they'll be pre-added as Going when the game is created.

- Tap **Select All** to invite everyone at once.
- Tap the 👑 crown icon next to a selected member to make them a **co-host**.

---

### Recurring Games

On paid plans, you can schedule repeating sessions automatically.

1. While creating a game, scroll to the **Recurring Game** section.
2. Toggle it on.
3. Choose a frequency: **Weekly**, **Every 2 Weeks**, or **Monthly**.
4. Set the number of occurrences.
5. A preview of all scheduled dates will appear before you save.

> Recurring games create individual game entries — each one can be edited or cancelled independently.

---

### Viewing Upcoming Games

- **Home tab** — shows your next upcoming games at a glance.
- **Games tab** — shows all your games (upcoming and past), including group games and games you've joined as a guest.

---

### Viewing Game Details

Tap any game card to open the **Game Detail** screen. Here you'll find:
- Date, time, and location (with a **Directions** button)
- Host name and host notes
- RSVP counts (Going, Maybe, Not Going)
- Player list with avatars
- Waitlist (if applicable)
- Add to Calendar button
- Game Chat

---

### RSVP / Responding to a Game

On the Game Detail screen:
- Tap **Going** to confirm your attendance.
- Tap **Maybe** if you're unsure.
- Tap **Not Going** to decline.

Your response updates instantly for everyone in the group.

---

### Waitlists

When a game is full, you'll be offered the option to **join the waitlist**. If a spot opens up, you'll automatically move from the waitlist to the guest list.

- Tap **Join Waitlist** to add yourself.
- Tap **Leave Waitlist** to remove yourself.

---

### Editing a Game

Only the host (or co-host) can edit a game.

1. Open the game and tap the **✏️ edit** button in the top-right corner.
2. Update any details.
3. Tap **Save Changes**.

---

### Cancelling a Game

1. Open the game and tap **✏️** to enter edit mode.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Archive Game**.

> Archived games are removed from the active view but are not permanently deleted.

---

### Leaving a Game

1. Open the game detail screen.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Leave Game**.
3. Confirm when prompted.

---

### Seating Plan (Host Feature)

Hosts can auto-assign players to tables based on skill level.

1. Open the game and tap **Seating Plan**.
2. Tap **🔀 Randomize** to generate table assignments.
3. To swap two players, tap one to select them (they'll highlight), then tap the player to swap with.
4. The seating plan is saved automatically and visible to all players.

---

### Recording a Winner

After a game has passed, the host can record who won.

1. Open the past game.
2. Scroll to the **Winner** section and tap **Pick Winner**.
3. Tap the player's name.
4. The winner is displayed on the game card going forward.

---

### Adding a Game to Your Calendar

1. Open the game detail screen.
2. Scroll to the **Add to calendar** section.
3. Choose **Google Calendar** (opens in browser) or **Apple / Download .ics** (downloads a calendar file).

## 3. Groups

### Creating a Group

1. Tap **Groups** in the bottom navigation.
2. Tap **+ New Group** (or the menu icon → **New Group**).
3. Enter a **Group Name** (e.g., "Tuesday Tiles").
4. Choose a **Group Icon** from the emoji picker.
5. Optionally customize the **Group Code** (auto-generated if left blank).
6. Toggle **Open Invites** if you'd like anyone with the join code to be able to join without approval.
7. Tap **🎉 Create Group**.

> Your plan determines how many active groups you can have. Check **Account → Subscription** for your current limit.

---

### Editing a Group

1. Tap **Groups** → tap your group.
2. Tap the **✏️ edit** button in the top-right corner.
3. Update the name or icon.
4. Tap **Save Changes ✨**.

---

### Inviting Members to a Group

1. Open your group.
2. Tap the **invite icon** (person with a +) in the top-right corner.
3. Share the invite via **Text Message**, **Copy Message**, **Share...**, or show the **QR Code**.
4. Members can also join by entering the **Group Join Code** directly in the app.

---

### Viewing Group Members

Open your group to see a full list of members with their avatars and names. The host is indicated with a 👑 crown.

---

### Removing Members / Leaving a Group

**To leave a group yourself:**
1. Open the group and tap **Edit ✏️**.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Leave Group**.

**To transfer host before leaving:**
1. Tap **Edit ✏️** → **Transfer & Leave**.
2. Select the member who should become the new host.

---

### Archiving a Group

1. Open the group and tap **Edit ✏️**.
2. Tap **Archive Group**.
3. If there are upcoming games, you'll be asked how to handle them:
   - **Move Games & Archive Group** — games become standalone and stay active.
   - **Archive Group & Games** — everything is archived together.

---

### Group Privacy (Open vs Closed Invites)

When creating or editing a group, toggle **Open Invites**:
- **On** — anyone with the join link or code can join automatically.
- **Off** — only people you personally invite can join.

---

### Joining a Group

**With a join code:**
1. Tap **Groups** → the menu icon → **Join with code**.
2. Enter the group code and tap **Join Group**.

**With a QR code:**
1. Tap **Groups** → the menu icon → **Join with code**.
2. Tap **Scan QR** and point your camera at the QR code.

**With an invite link:**
- Tap a shared link and the app will open directly to the group join screen.

---

## 4. Chat & Messaging

### Group Chat

Each group has a dedicated group chat for all members.

1. Open your group.
2. Tap the **💬 chat bubble** button.
3. Type your message and tap **Send** (or press Enter).

---

### Game Chat

Every game has its own chat for game-specific conversation.

1. Open any game.
2. Tap the **💬** button in the top-right corner.
3. Type your message and send.

---

### Reactions

In any chat, tap the **😊 emoji** button next to a message to add a reaction. Tap the same reaction to remove it. Reaction counts are visible to everyone.

---

### Replies

Tap **Reply** below any message to respond directly to it. Replies are nested under the original message.

---

### Notification Banner in Chat

If you haven't enabled push notifications, a banner may appear at the top of the chat. Tap **Enable** to allow notifications and stay updated.

---

## 5. Invitations

### Inviting Players to a Game

1. Open the game you want to invite to.
2. Tap the **invite icon** (person with a +) in the top-right corner.
3. Choose how to share:
   - **Text Message** — opens your SMS app with a pre-written invite.
   - **Copy Message** — copies the full invite text to your clipboard.
   - **Share...** — opens your device's share sheet for all sharing options.
4. Alternatively, show the **QR Code** for someone to scan in person.
5. Or share the **Game Join Code** — a 6-character code players enter in the app.

---

### Inviting Players to a Group

1. Open your group.
2. Tap the **invite icon** in the top-right corner.
3. Share via Text Message, Copy Message, Share, or the QR code.
4. The **Group Join Code** is displayed at the bottom for easy sharing.

---

### Accepting an Invitation

**Via a shared link:**
- Tap the link. The app opens and adds you to the game or group automatically.

**Via a game code:**
1. Tap **Groups** → menu → **Join with code**.
2. Enter the 6-character game code.
3. Tap **Join** — you'll be RSVP'd as Going immediately.

**Via a QR code:**
1. Tap **Groups** → menu → **Join with code**.
2. Tap **Scan QR** and aim your camera at the QR code.

---

### Declining an Invitation

If you received an invite link to a game, tap the link and RSVP as **Not Going** after you're added to the game.

---

## 6. Notifications

### Enabling Push Notifications

1. Tap **Account** in the bottom navigation.
2. In the **Notifications** section, tap the toggle next to **Push Notifications**.
3. When prompted, tap **Allow** to grant permission.

Once enabled, you'll receive alerts for:
- New messages in group and game chats
- Game updates and changes

---

### Disabling Notifications

1. Tap **Account** → **Notifications**.
2. Tap the **Push Notifications** toggle to turn it off.

---

### Notifications on iPhone

Push notifications on iPhone require the app to be installed to your Home Screen:

1. Open Mahjong Club in **Safari** (not Chrome).
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add** in the top-right corner.
5. Open the app from your Home Screen, then enable notifications in **Account**.

> Chrome on iPhone does not support push notifications. Use Safari.

---

### If Notifications Stop Working

If the app shows notifications as enabled but you're not receiving them, your device may have revoked permission.

1. Tap **Account** → **Notifications**.
2. If you see a warning about revoked permissions, tap **Fix →**.
3. Follow the on-screen prompts to re-grant permission.

Or check your device settings: **Settings → Mahjong Club → Notifications → Allow Notifications**.

## 7. Profile & Preferences

### Editing Your Display Name

1. Tap **Account** → **Edit ✏️** in the My Profile card.
2. Update the **Display Name** field.
3. Tap **Save Changes ✨**.

---

### Choosing an Avatar

Mahjong Club uses emoji avatars — no photo required.

1. Tap **Account** → **Edit ✏️**.
2. In the **Avatar** section, tap any emoji to select it.
3. Each avatar has a unique jewel-tone color that's used throughout the app.
4. Tap **Save Changes ✨**.

**Available themes include:** mahjong tiles, bamboo, florals, cocktails, elegant nature, and luxury symbols.

---

### Setting Your Skill Level

1. Tap **Account** → **Edit ✏️**.
2. Tap your skill level: **Beginner 🌱**, **Intermediate 🀄**, or **Advanced 🏆**.
3. Tap **Save Changes ✨**.

> Your skill level helps the host's seating plan create balanced tables.

---

### Changing Your Timezone

1. Tap **Account** → **Edit ✏️**.
2. Tap the **Timezone** dropdown and select your timezone.
3. Tap **Save Changes ✨**.

Game dates and times are always shown in your local timezone.

---

### Changing the App Theme

1. Tap **Account** and scroll to the **Appearance** section.
2. Choose from four themes:
   - 🌸 **Flowers** — warm pinks and plums (default)
   - 🐦 **Bam Bird** — soft greens and forest tones
   - 🐉 **Dragons** — deep indigo and jewel tones
   - 💠 **Tiles** — cool blues and sky tones
3. The theme updates instantly — no save needed.

---

### Installing the App (Web)

For the best experience on your phone, install Mahjong Club to your Home Screen.

**On iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open [ourmahjong.club](https://ourmahjong.club) in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** icon → **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the site in Chrome.
2. Tap **Account** → **Add to Home Screen** → **Install**.

## 8. Troubleshooting & FAQ

---

**Q: I'm not receiving push notifications.**

A: A few things to check:
- Make sure notifications are enabled in **Account → Notifications**.
- On iPhone, the app must be installed to your Home Screen via Safari. Chrome on iPhone does not support push notifications.
- Check your device settings: **Settings → Mahjong Club → Notifications** and make sure "Allow Notifications" is on.
- If the app shows a warning about revoked permissions, tap **Fix →** in the Notifications section of your Account.

---

**Q: I can't join a game — the join code isn't working.**

A: Double-check the code carefully — the game join code uses only letters and numbers (no ambiguous characters like O, 0, I, or l). Make sure you're entering it in **Groups → Join with code**. If the code was for a past game, it may have expired.

---

**Q: I can't create a new group — it says I've hit my limit.**

A: Your subscription plan limits the number of active groups you can have. To check your usage, go to **Account → Subscription**. You can archive an existing group to free up a slot, or upgrade your plan to add more groups.

---

**Q: I can't schedule a new game — it says I've hit my limit.**

A: Your plan includes a limit on upcoming hosted games. Archive or let a scheduled game pass to free up a slot, or upgrade your plan for more capacity.

---

**Q: How do I sign in with Google instead of email?**

A: On the Sign In screen, tap **Sign in with Google**. This works on both the web app and the iOS app.

---

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**

A: On the Sign In screen, tap **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and tap send. You'll receive a reset email shortly. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.

---

**Q: What's the difference between the web app and the iOS app?**

A: They're the same app — same features, same data. The iOS app from the App Store is installed directly on your device. The web app runs in your browser at [ourmahjong.club](https://ourmahjong.club). You can also install the web app to your Home Screen for a near-native experience on both iPhone and Android.

Push notifications behave slightly differently: on iOS, they require the Safari Home Screen app or the native iOS app. On Android and desktop, the web app supports notifications in Chrome and Firefox.

---

**Q: How do I update the app?**

- **iOS app:** Updates come through the App Store automatically, or go to the App Store and tap **Update** next to Mahjong Club.
- **Web app:** It updates automatically — just refresh the page if you think you're on an older version.

---

**Q: Someone invited me to a game but I'm not in the group — can I still join?**

A: Yes! When you receive a game invite link or join code, you can RSVP to that specific game as a guest, even without being a full group member. You'll see the game in your **Games** tab after joining.

---

**Q: What does "Maybe" mean for my RSVP?**

A: "Maybe" lets the host know you might attend but aren't confirmed. It's helpful for the host when planning seating and player counts. The host can still see who said Maybe in the game's player tally.

---

**Q: Can I turn off notifications just for one group or game?**

A: Currently the notification toggle in **Account** controls all push notifications. You can always mute your device or turn notifications off and back on at any time.

---

**Q: How does the seating plan work?**

A: The host can open the Seating Plan from the game detail screen. Tapping **Randomize** auto-assigns all confirmed players across the available tables, with an attempt to balance skill levels. You can then manually swap any two players by tapping them. The seating plan is saved and visible to all players once the host generates it.
