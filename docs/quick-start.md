# Quick Start Guide — Mahjong Club

Welcome to Mahjong Club — the simplest way to schedule, manage, and enjoy your Mahjong games. This guide gets you set up in minutes.

---

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

1. Open the app and tap **Create Account**.
2. Enter your name, email address, and a password, then tap **Sign Up**.
3. Alternatively, tap **Continue with Google** to sign in with your Google account.
4. Tap through the welcome screens to finish setup.

> You're in. Your profile is ready to go.

---

## Step 2 — Personalize Your Profile

1. Tap the **Account** tab (bottom right).
2. Tap **Edit Profile** to set your display name and choose an avatar emoji.
3. Set your **Skill Level** (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced) — the app uses this to balance seating plans.
4. Pick a **Theme** if you'd like to change the look: Flowers 🌸, Bam Bird 🐦, Dragons 🐉, or Tiles 💠.

---

## Step 3 — Join or Create a Group

**To join an existing group:**
1. Tap the **Groups** tab.
2. Tap **Join Group** and enter the group's join code, or ask a member to invite you directly.

**To start your own group:**
1. Tap the **Groups** tab, then **+**.
2. Give your group a name and choose an emoji.
3. Share the join code or invite link with your players.

---

## Step 4 — Schedule a Game

1. Tap the **Games** tab, then **+**.
2. Enter the game title, date, start time, and location.
3. Choose which group the game belongs to.
4. Set the number of tables and invite specific members — or leave it open for your group.
5. Tap **Create Game**.

Players in your group will be notified automatically.

---

## Step 5 — Respond to an Invitation

When someone invites you to a game, you'll receive a push notification. Tap it to open the game, then choose:

- **Going** — you're in
- **Maybe** — you might attend
- **Can't Go** — you're not available

You can change your response any time before the game.

---

## What's Next?

| Want to… | Go to… |
|---|---|
| Chat with your group | Groups tab → your group → **Chat** |
| See who's coming to a game | Games tab → tap the game |
| Invite someone new | Games tab → game → **Invite** button |
| View seating assignments | Game detail → **Seating** (host sets this up) |
| Enable push notifications | Account tab → **Notifications** |
