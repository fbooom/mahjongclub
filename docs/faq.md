# Frequently Asked Questions — Mahjong Club

---

## Account & Sign-In

**Can I use the app without creating an account?**
No — an account is required to join groups, RSVP to games, and chat. You can sign up with an email address or with your Google account.

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password?**, enter your email address, and follow the link sent to your inbox. If you signed up with Google, sign in through Google instead — there is no separate app password.

**Can I change my email address?**
Email changes are not currently supported in the app. If you need to switch accounts, sign out and create a new account.

**Can I delete my account?**
Account deletion is not yet available in the app. Contact your group host or reach out to the app administrator for assistance.

---

## Groups

**How do I join a group?**
Ask a group member for the join code or invite link. You can also be invited directly by the group host.

**What's the difference between an open and closed group?**
With **Open Invites** enabled, anyone who has the join code or link can join without waiting for host approval. With it disabled, joining requires a direct invitation from the host.

**Can I belong to more than one group?**
Yes — you can be a member of multiple groups at the same time.

**How do I leave a group?**
Open the group, tap the menu (⋯), and select **Leave Group**.

**Can a host remove me from a group?**
Yes, hosts can remove members from their group at any time.

**What does archiving a group do?**
An archived group is no longer active — no new games can be scheduled and it no longer appears in your main groups list. Archived groups can be restored by the host.

## Games & Scheduling

**Who can create a game?**
Any group member can create a game within a group they belong to.

**Can I invite people outside my group to a game?**
Yes — hosts can share an invite link, join code, or QR code that allows guests to join a specific game directly.

**What is a join code?**
A 6-character code unique to each game. Share it with anyone you want to invite — they enter it under **Join a Game** to join directly without needing the group first.

**What are co-hosts?**
Co-hosts share the host's management permissions for a game: they can edit details, manage RSVPs, set up seating, and record winners.

**Can I edit a game after I create it?**
Yes — the game host and co-hosts can edit any game detail before or after the game.

**How do I cancel a game?**
Open the game, tap **Edit**, and select **Delete Game**. All invited players will be removed from the game.

**What is the waitlist?**
If a game reaches its player limit, additional RSVPs go onto a waitlist. If a spot opens up, waitlisted players move up automatically.

**What are recurring games?**
Recurring games let you schedule the same game on a repeating schedule (weekly, biweekly, or monthly). This feature is available on select subscription plans.

**How do I export a game to my calendar?**
Open the game detail and tap **Add to Calendar**. You can add it to Google Calendar or download an `.ics` file for Apple Calendar or Outlook.

## RSVP & Attendance

**Can I change my RSVP?**
Yes — open the game and tap a different response (Going, Maybe, or Can't Go) any time before the game starts.

**What happens if a game is full?**
You'll be placed on the waitlist automatically. If a spot opens, you'll move up and receive a notification.

**Does the host see who RSVPed?**
Yes — the host and co-hosts see each player's response on the game detail page.

---

## Seating Plans

**Who sets up the seating plan?**
Only the game host or a co-host can generate and publish the seating plan.

**How does the app assign seats?**
The app uses each player's skill level to distribute players as evenly as possible across tables. It does not place two players of the same skill level together when a balanced arrangement is possible.

**Can I swap seats?**
Yes — after the seating plan is generated, the host can manually drag players between seats before publishing.

**Can players see the seating plan?**
Yes — once the host publishes it, all confirmed players can see their assigned table.

## Chat

**Is chat available for every game and group?**
Yes — every group has its own chat, and every game has its own game chat.

**Can I react to messages?**
Yes — tap and hold a message to add an emoji reaction.

**Can I reply to a specific message?**
Yes — swipe right on any message or tap the reply icon to respond directly to it.

## Invitations

**How do I invite someone to a game?**
Open the game, tap the **Invite** button, and choose how to share: via SMS, a copied link, the system share sheet, or a QR code.

**What is the QR code for?**
The QR code on the invite screen encodes the game's join link. Another person can scan it with their phone camera or the in-app scanner to join directly.

**Can someone join a game without having the app?**
They'll need the app to RSVP and participate fully. The join link will prompt them to download it if they don't have it yet.

## Notifications

**Why am I not receiving push notifications?**
Check that notifications are enabled in two places:
1. **In the app** — Account tab → Notifications toggle should be on.
2. **On your device** — iOS or Android settings → Mahjong Club → Notifications should be allowed.

**I use Chrome on iPhone and notifications aren't working.**
Chrome on iPhone does not support web push notifications. Use Safari on iPhone for notification support, or install the app from Safari as a PWA (Add to Home Screen).

**How do I stop getting notifications for a specific game or group?**
Per-game and per-group notification muting is not currently available. To stop all notifications, turn off the Notifications toggle in your Account settings.

## Themes & Appearance

**How do I change the app theme?**
Account tab → scroll to **Theme** → tap your preferred theme. Changes apply immediately.

**What themes are available?**
Flowers 🌸, Bam Bird 🐦, Dragons 🐉, and Tiles 💠.

## Troubleshooting

**The app seems stuck or is showing old data.**
Pull down on any list to refresh, or close and reopen the app.

**I can't connect to the app.**
Check your internet connection. The app requires a live connection to load and update game data.

**Someone's name isn't showing correctly.**
Each member controls their own display name. Ask them to update it in their Account settings.

**The seating plan button isn't showing up.**
Seating plans are only available to the host and co-hosts of a game. If you're a regular member, you'll see the plan once the host publishes it.

**I entered the wrong RSVP — can I change it?**
Yes — tap a different option (Going, Maybe, Can't Go) on the game detail page. Your response updates immediately.
