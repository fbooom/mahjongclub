Refund Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 15, 2025

We offer full refunds within 14 days of any charge. No questions asked.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if you request it within 14 days of the date you were charged. This applies to all paid subscription plans, including the Pro and Club plans.

Refund requests submitted after the 14-day window will not be eligible for a refund, and the subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

2. Free Trials

Paid plans that include a free trial period are not billed during the trial. No refund is necessary if you cancel before the trial ends — you will not be charged.

If you are charged at the end of your trial and wish to cancel, you are eligible for a full refund if the request is made within 14 days of that charge.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us by email at:

ourmjclub@gmail.com

Please include the following in your message:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • The date of the charge you are requesting a refund for
  • The reason for your request (optional but appreciated)

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

4. Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been issued.

5. Subscription Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription and requesting a refund are two separate actions.

  • Cancellation stops future charges and downgrades your account to the Free plan at the end of the current billing period (or immediately, at your request).
  • A refund reverses a charge that has already been made. You must request this separately within 14 days of the charge.

To cancel your subscription, go to Account → Manage Subscription → Cancel subscription within the app.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Charges older than 14 days from the date of the request
  • Accounts that have been suspended or terminated for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Charges resulting from failure to cancel before the end of a free trial, where the trial end date was clearly disclosed at the time of sign-up

7. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. We are committed to resolving disputes directly and can typically process refunds faster than a chargeback dispute. Accounts with open chargebacks may be temporarily suspended while the dispute is under review.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy or need help with a refund, please reach out at ourmjclub@gmail.com. We're happy to help.
